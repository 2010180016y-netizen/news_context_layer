What the extension does

News Context opens a side panel beside a supported news article and helps users compare related coverage from other publishers.

Data we process

  • article URL or canonical URL
  • article title, publisher, author names, timestamps, section, keywords
  • a short article excerpt used for comparison
  • parse confidence and related matching signals
  • anonymous device identifiers, watchlist state, and plan state
  • analytics events only when the user explicitly opts in

Advertising

We do not use article data or usage data for targeted advertising, and we do not sell personal information.

Analytics

Analytics is optional and opt-in. If enabled, it is used only to improve product quality and reliability. If disabled, analytics events are not sent.

Local storage

The extension stores limited local state in the browser, such as onboarding completion, analytics preference, anonymous device identifiers, latest panel context, and checkout return state.

Support and operational contact

Support/contact routes may be configured separately for alpha operations. If no live support route is configured, the extension keeps support actions on a safe fallback notice instead of opening a broken link.

Your choices

Users can choose whether to opt in to analytics, stop using the extension at any time, and contact the operator using the public contact route listed above.
